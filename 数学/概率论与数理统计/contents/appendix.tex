        0-1分布 & $B(1,p)$ & $P\{X=k\}=p^k (1-p)^{1-k}, \; k=0,1, \; 0<p<1$ & $p$ & $p(1-p)$ \\

        \rule{0pt}{24pt}几何分布 & - & $P\{X=k\}=(1-p)^{k-1} p, \; k=1,2,\cdots$ & $\dfrac{1}{p}$ & $\dfrac{1-p}{p^2}$ \\[8pt]
